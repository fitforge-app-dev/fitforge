Programs by Age
EVERY AGE, EVERY STAGE
13–17
Teens — Building foundation
Focus on bodyweight movements, correct form, and flexible mobility. Avoid heavy compound lifts until bones are fully developed. 3x/week full body workouts with emphasis on fun and consistency.
18–30
Prime — Peak muscle building
Highest testosterone and recovery speed. Ideal phase for heavy lifting, progressive overload, and body recomposition. Push hard — 4-5x/week with PPL or Upper/Lower splits.
31–50
Adult — Maintain & strengthen
Recovery is slower; joint health becomes priority. Include mobility work, deload weeks, and sufficient sleep. Still make great gains with smart programming. 4x/week recommended.
51+
Senior — Longevity & strength
Resistance training fights sarcopenia (muscle loss). Lower weight, higher reps, focus on balance and functional strength. Yoga and mobility sessions 1-2x/week alongside 3x resistance training.
